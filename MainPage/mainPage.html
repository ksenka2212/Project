Alcohol Calculator

You have come to a site that will allow you to cope with difficult calculations. Alcohol Calculator is the best way to accurately estimate your blood alcohol level online. Using its advanced algorithm, Drunk Calc can predict how drunk you are.

  • Instruction
  • To use the alcohol calculator, you need to enter: gender, weight, height of a person, the degree of fullness of the stomach, strength and amount of alcohol consumed. Then click on the "Calculate" button. The calculator will calculate the maximum concentration of ethyl alcohol, the degree of intoxication and the time until alcohol is completely eliminated from the body.
